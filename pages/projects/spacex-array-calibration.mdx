**Spring 2024 – Fall 2024 · RF Engineering Internship**

The job was to automate phased-array calibration workflows that were taking hours to run by hand. Working across RF, validation, and software teams under real production deadlines, I built Python tooling that replaced a 3-hour manual process with something that finished in 30 seconds.

That's not a rounding error — it's the kind of speedup that changes what's actually possible at production scale. Getting there meant understanding the measurement hardware well enough to know what could be parallelized, what had to stay sequential, and where the existing process was just slow by habit.
