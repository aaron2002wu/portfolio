**Spring 2024 – Fall 2024 · RF Engineering Internship**

Automated phased-array calibration workflows that previously took hours to complete manually.
Working across RF, validation, and software teams under production deadlines, I built Python
tooling that reduced a three-hour process to roughly 30 seconds.

The improvement materially changed what was practical at production scale. Delivering that result
required understanding the measurement hardware well enough to identify what could be parallelized,
what needed to remain sequential, and where the existing workflow was constrained more by habit
than by technical limits.
