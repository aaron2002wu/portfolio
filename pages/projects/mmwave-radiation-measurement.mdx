_Fall 2022 – Spring 2023_

Built and fabricated hardware mounts for a robotic antenna-measurement system used in a 6-110 GHz research setup.

## What I Did

- Designed custom mounts for stable antenna positioning across repeated trials.
- Worked with near-to-far-field measurement workflows to support accurate pattern analysis.
- Coordinated with lab researchers to validate fit, robustness, and repeatability.

## Outcome

The mount design remains in active lab use as core infrastructure for ongoing RF measurement research.
