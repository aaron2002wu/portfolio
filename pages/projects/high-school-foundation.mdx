**2017 – 2021**

High school during COVID meant most of what you'd built toward just stopped. Competitions canceled, schools closed, normal handoffs between graduating and incoming leadership gone.

I was president of both Science Olympiad and Science National Honor Society when that happened. Mostly it meant figuring out how to keep things running when the usual structure wasn't there — coordinating members remotely, keeping the chapter active, and finishing out a senior year that looked nothing like anyone planned.

It wasn't glamorous, but running organizations through that period taught me something real about ownership. When there's no infrastructure to lean on, you either build something or nothing happens. That instinct shows up in everything I've worked on since.
