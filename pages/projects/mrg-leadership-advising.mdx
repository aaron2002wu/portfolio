_Fall 2021 – Oct 2024_

Joined the Georgia Tech Marine Robotics Group in freshman year and later served in leadership roles
including Electrical Lead and President from December 2022 through October 2024. In that capacity,
I helped shape onboarding, technical advising, systems integration, and field-test execution across
multiple student competition teams.

## What I Did

- Built onboarding structure and integration standards so new members could contribute more quickly.
- Led electrical, compute, and systems-integration efforts across surface and underwater platforms.
- Coordinated field-test planning, safety reviews, readiness checks, and cross-team troubleshooting.
- Advised students working on competition platforms including RoboBoat, RoboSub, RobotX, and Stinger Tug.

## Outcome

Improved technical readiness, continuity, and execution quality across multiple marine robotics platforms.
