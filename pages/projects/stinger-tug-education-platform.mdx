_Fall 2024 – Present_

Led integration and field testing for the Stinger Tug autonomous surface vehicle as part of the
Georgia Tech Marine Robotics Group. As President, Electrical Lead, and Systems Lead, I used the
platform as both a technical testbed and an onboarding environment for newer team members.

## What I Did

- Directed subsystem integration across sensing, compute, and control.
- Established repeatable field-test checklists and safety workflows.
- Mentored members on deployment readiness and systems debugging.
- Helped turn the platform into a reliable educational vehicle for hands-on systems learning.

## Outcome

Created a reliable platform for onboarding, mentoring, and real-world validation in student-led
marine robotics work.
