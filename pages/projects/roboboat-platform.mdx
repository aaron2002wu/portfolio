_2023 - Present_

Supported the RoboBoat competition platform through power systems, networking, and systems
integration work, then expanded into advising students and contributing to autonomy-focused
software efforts.

## What I Did

- Integrated electrical and networking subsystems to improve deployment reliability.
- Helped prepare the platform for iterative on-water testing and competition-oriented development.
- Mentored students during debugging, integration, and field-readiness work.

## Outcome

Created a more reliable platform for repeated autonomy testing and team handoff between members.
