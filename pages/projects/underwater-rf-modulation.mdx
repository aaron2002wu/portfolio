# Perfect-Pulse Underwater RF Modulation Study

Studied underwater RF propagation behavior and proposed a novel modulation concept for improved power efficiency.

## What I Did

- Modeled transfer functions between submerged antennas in HFSS.
- Analyzed propagation constraints for underwater communication channels.
- Framed and tested a modulation concept tied to measured channel behavior.

## Outcome

Work contributed to publication activity and earned both a PURA award and best poster recognition at IEEE DTPI 2023.
