_Summer 2023_

During the Scripps MPL REU, integrated side-scan sonar sensing into an autonomous surface vehicle stack.

## What I Did

- Implemented sonar data integration for navigation experiments.
- Supported ocean field-test operations and data capture sessions.
- Organized collected datasets for downstream localization validation.

## Outcome

Generated field datasets that supported validation experiments for novel localization methods.
