_Fall 2024 – Present_

Designed a containerized ROS 2 architecture for perception, navigation, and control services across
heterogeneous robotic systems in the Aerospace Systems Design Lab. The work emphasizes reproducible
deployment, multi-platform integration, and hardware-in-the-loop validation for field robotics.

## Why This Matters

Robotics teams move faster when deployment is consistent, debuggable, and portable across platforms.
This architecture makes it easier to understand how services are isolated, connected, and validated
in a real multi-domain system.
